Korea to Provide Humanitarian Assistance to People of Ukraine

1\. The Korean government has decided to provide US$10 million in emergency humanitarian assistance to help the Ukrainian government, people, and refugees facing a severe crisis due to the illegal invasion by Russia.

The humanitarian assistance will be delivered promptly after consultations with the Ukrainian government and its neighboring countries as well as international organizations.
