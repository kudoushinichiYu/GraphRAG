WFP Ukraine and Neighbouring Countries Situation Report #4, 10 March 2022

**In Numbers:**

- 18 million people affected 

- 30 percent of Ukraine population in need of life-saving assistance 

- 2.3 million people displaced to neighbouring countries

**Highlights:**

- WFP established its presence inside Ukraine and joining forces with local partners to deliver much-needed assistance 

- Close to 20,000 mt of food commodities are en route to Ukraine and neighbouring countries
