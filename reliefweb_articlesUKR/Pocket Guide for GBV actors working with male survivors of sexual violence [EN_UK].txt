Pocket Guide for GBV actors working with male survivors of sexual violence [EN/UK]

PURPOSE OF THE DOCUMENT: This document aims to provide guidance for those working with male survivors of sexual violence. It emphasizes the importance of recognizing and addressing the specific needs of this group, including the barriers they may face in accessing services. The guide also highlights the need for sensitivity and understanding when interacting with male survivors and offers key messages and communication strategies to ensure their experiences are validated and respected.
