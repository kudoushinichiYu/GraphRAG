Exchange of Notes concerning Grant Aid to Ukraine “the Programme for Emergency Recovery (Phase 4)”

On February 27, 2025, H.E. Mr.NAKAGOME Masashi, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Ukraine and Oleksii KULEBA, Deputy Prime Minister for Restoration of Ukraine – Minister for Development of Communities and Territories of Ukraine signed and exchanged notes for the Grant Aid “the Programme for Emergency Recovery (Phase 4)” (grant amount: 8.8 billion yen) in Ukraine.

This cooperation will provide the Government of Ukraine, which has been suffering from Russia’s aggression, with assistance for (1) early recovery from war damage (support for energy sector and others) and (2) inclusive reconstruction (support for local governments and economic revitalization). This will thereby contribute to the rapid recovery and reconstruction of Ukraine.
