UNHCR Greece Factsheet - February 2025

UNHCR works with the authorities to ensure a favourable protection space and developing sustainable responses for refugees, asylum-seekers and stateless people.

UNHCR supports asylum-seekers and refugees with provision of information on rights and responsibilities, counselling, legal aid, referrals to services and livelihoods programmes.

UNHCR offers legal advice and technical expertise to the Government on asylum, reception and durable solutions, including on the implementation of the EU Pact
