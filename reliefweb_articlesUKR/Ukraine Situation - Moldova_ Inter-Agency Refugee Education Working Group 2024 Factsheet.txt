Ukraine Situation - Moldova: Inter-Agency Refugee Education Working Group 2024 Factsheet

The Refugee Education Working Group (REWG) aims to strengthen coordination among education stakeholders in Moldova to support the Government in providing quality, inclusive education in safe and protective environments for refugee children. Led by the Ministry of Education and Research (MER), co-led by UNICEF and UNHCR, the group offers technical support, strategic policy guidance, and capacity development to education stakeholders.
